Personal project

Sakura

A single-file workspace for outlining, meeting notes, a decision log, mind maps, and architecture diagrams — linked to each other, not just co-located. Promote a meeting action item and it becomes a real to-do, due date inherited and linked back to its source note. The same document presents live in meetings — no PowerPoint needed — and exports to Word, PDF, or slides once it's ready to share.

It also has AI built in — rewrite, expand, generate outlines, extract action items, and more — powered by your own API key across seven providers, so nothing ever runs through a server Sakura controls. Built solo over 33 days — evenings and weekends around a full-time job — with Claude as a development partner. Architecture, security review, and every final call mine, with AI accelerating the build. Actively maintained and hardened since, including a full security audit — Content-Security-Policy, Subresource Integrity on every third-party script, and stored-XSS remediation.

No install, no account, all local — everything lives in your browser.
